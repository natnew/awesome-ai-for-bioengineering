# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming, professional space for everyone who contributes to or uses the list. We are committed to a harassment-free experience for all, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, constructive, and concise.
- Focus discussion on the technical merit and relevance of resources.
- Assume good faith and accept review feedback graciously.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Submitting unsafe biological procedural detail (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)).
- Spam, promotional submissions, or deliberate degradation of list quality.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, decline, or remove contributions, issues, and comments that violate this Code of Conduct. To report a concern, open an issue or contact the maintainer directly. Reports will be handled discreetly.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
