# Security and safety boundaries

This repository is a curated index of AI engineering resources relevant to bioengineering. It is **not** a clinical, medical, laboratory, or experimental-protocol resource.

## Scope of content

The list links to external papers, repositories, datasets, benchmarks, tools, institutions, and high-level safety and governance resources. It provides:

- technical summaries at the level of AI systems and what they model, predict, or generate;
- links to canonical sources;
- AI engineering analysis, evaluation framing, and responsible release context.

## Out of scope

This repository must not contain:

- wet-lab or experimental protocols;
- pathogen engineering or enhancement guidance;
- genetic-manipulation procedures;
- chemical or biological synthesis routes;
- any operational detail that could enable unsafe biological experimentation.

Entries that drift toward operational detail will be generalised or removed.

## Responsible release framing

Where resources involve dual-use considerations, the list favours sources that document responsible release, controlled access, or biosecurity review. The list is intended for technical research mapping and AI engineering awareness.

## Reporting a concern

If you believe an entry crosses the safety boundary, includes unsafe procedural detail, or links to inappropriate material:

- open a **Correction** issue describing the affected entry and the concern, or
- contact the maintainer directly.

Please do not include unsafe detail in the report itself. A short description of the problem is sufficient.

## Repository security

This repository contains documentation and configuration only; it ships no executable application code. Report any workflow or supply-chain concerns via the issue tracker.
