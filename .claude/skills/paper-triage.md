# Skill: paper-triage

Decide whether a paper belongs in the list and where.

## Steps

1. **Identify** — Title, authors, venue, year, and a stable identifier (arXiv abstract or DOI).
2. **Relevance** — Does it advance AI engineering for bioengineering (method, model, evaluation, or responsible release)?
3. **Signal** — Is it foundational, widely used, or a clear reference point? Avoid incremental or low-impact papers.
4. **Section** — Map to the best section (often Foundations and Surveys, a modelling section, Benchmarks and Evaluation, Reproducibility and Validation, or Safety, Security, and Responsible Release).
5. **Canonical link** — Prefer the DOI or arXiv abstract page over a hosted PDF.
6. **Safety** — Ensure the entry can be summarised without operational biological detail.

## Output

- Decision: include / exclude, with reason.
- If include, the entry and target section.
