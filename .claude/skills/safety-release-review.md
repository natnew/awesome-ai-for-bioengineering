# Skill: safety-release-review

Review safety and responsible-release resources at a high level, without adding unsafe detail.

## Steps

1. **Identify** — The resource and its source (institution, initiative, policy document, or paper).
2. **Relevance** — Does it inform responsible development, release, or governance of AI for bioengineering?
3. **Level** — Confirm it is governance- or framework-level, not operational. Do not summarise any procedural biological content.
4. **Framing** — Describe the resource in terms of responsible release, controlled access, biosecurity, or evaluation.
5. **Placement** — Usually Safety, Security, and Responsible Release.

## Output

- Decision: include / exclude, with reason.
- If include, a one-line neutral entry that stays within the safety boundary.
- Never reproduce or paraphrase unsafe procedural detail in the output.
